NovaBank Product FAQ

Q: How do I set up direct deposit?
A: Provide your employer with your NovaBank account and routing number,
found in the mobile app under Account Details.

Q: Does NovaBank charge foreign transaction fees?
A: Basic and Plus accounts incur a 2.5% foreign transaction fee. Premier
accounts have no foreign transaction fees.

Q: Can I open a joint account?
A: Yes, joint accounts can be opened in any branch or through the mobile
app under Account Settings > Add Joint Owner.

Q: What is the daily ATM withdrawal limit?
A: The standard daily ATM withdrawal limit is $500 for Basic and Plus
accounts, and $1,000 for Premier accounts. Limits can be adjusted
temporarily through the mobile app.

Q: How do I close my account?
A: Visit a branch in person or call support. Accounts with a balance must
be zeroed out before closure.
