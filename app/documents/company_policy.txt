NovaBank Customer Support Policy

NovaBank customer support is available Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to
8:00 PM Eastern Time, and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM Eastern Time.
We are closed on Sundays and all federal holidays.

Refund requests for account fees are processed within 5-7 business days
of approval. Customers may dispute a transaction within 60 days of the
transaction date by contacting support or using the mobile app's dispute
feature.

Lost or stolen cards should be reported immediately through the mobile
app's "Freeze Card" feature or by calling the 24/7 card services line.
A replacement card typically arrives within 5-10 business days.

NovaBank offers three account tiers: Basic (no monthly fee), Plus ($9.99/month,
waived with $2,000 minimum balance), and Premier ($19.99/month, waived with
$10,000 minimum balance).
